An AI-powered storybook generator that creates personalized stories for children.

Problem Statement

Parents often struggle to find new and engaging stories for their children. I wanted to create a tool that could generate unique and personalized storybooks based on a child's interests and preferences.

Tech Stack

  • Flutter
  • Firebase
  • OpenAI GPT-3

Architecture

The app uses a Flutter front-end to gather input from the user, such as the child's name, interests, and desired story length. This information is then sent to a back-end service that uses OpenAI's GPT-3 to generate a story. The generated story is then displayed in a beautifully formatted storybook layout.

My Role

I was the sole developer for this project. I was responsible for the UI/UX design, front-end development, and integration with the OpenAI API.

Key Challenges

  • Prompt engineering to generate high-quality and age-appropriate stories.
  • Designing a user-friendly and engaging storybook interface.
  • Managing the costs associated with using the OpenAI API.

Lessons Learned

This project was a great introduction to the world of generative AI. I learned a lot about prompt engineering, working with large language models, and building AI-powered applications. I also gained valuable experience in Flutter and mobile app development.
